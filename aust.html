Ahsanullah University of Science and Technology (Bengali: ??????????? ??????? ? ????????? ??????????????) or commonly known as AUST is the first private engineering university to be established in Bangladesh. The University is considered as one of the finest engineering universities in Bangladesh, which was founded by the Dhaka Ahsania Mission in 1995. Dhaka Ahsania Mission is a non-profit voluntary organization in Bangladesh. The Mission was established in 1958 by Khan Bahadur Ahsanullah.
